Gadhe ko ghoda banane ki process jispe sadiyon se research kar rahe hain log usmein modern times mein to bilkul hi-fi solution hai. Achchi achchi quality ke engine oils available hain market mein jinhe pi ke gadha khud ko ghoda feel kar sakta hai. Iske alawa purane zamaane mein bhi bahut saari tarah ke oils available hote they jinse gadha khud ko ghoda feel kar sakta tha..... Ghoda hone ka alag hi mazaa hai.......

Jo Laal Ghoda Engine Oil Rajat Kalia ne(Uske Left Striker ne) piya tha Ghoda banne ke liye wo hadh se zyada refined quality ka tha........usmein tikkiyan bani huyi thi jis kaaran wo easily piya jaa paaya..........par asli raaz to pata nahi kise pata hoga ke usne kya piya tha........ Wo poori kenni muh laga ke pi gaya tha........

Gowdi ne pilaya tha FYI

Matlab oils to available hote hain bahut saare par kam hi logon ne hi piya hai shaayad jis kaaran kaafi kam log hi gadhe ki hybrid quality hone ka claim kar paa rahe hain....... zyada logon ne kyon nahi piya hai jabke saaf tour pe kuch log bol rahe hain ke Rajat Kalia is tarah bana tha ghoda yeh raaz aaj bhi raaz hi hai..........

Aam logon ko oil pee ke kya kuch compliations ka saamna karna padta hai kya yeh koi bhi theek se bol ke bata nahi paa raha aur poori oil mistrey raaz hi ban ke reh gayi hai bilkul hi..........
