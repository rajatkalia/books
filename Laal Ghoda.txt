Gadhe ko ghoda banane ki process jispe sadiyon se research kar rahe hain log usmein modern times mein to bilkul hi-fi solution hai. Iske alawa purane zamaane mein bhi bahut saari tarah ke oils available hote they jinse gadha khud ko ghoda feel kar sakta tha..... Ghoda hone ka alag hi mazaa hai.......
