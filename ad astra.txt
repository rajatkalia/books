Jaise ke aap jaante hi hain ke golgappe khane se bachcha paida hota hai......usi tarah advertisement dekhne se bhi bachcha paida hota hai....... halanke kuch log ismein kehte hain ke agar advertisement allow karo to bachcha paida hota hai jaise youtube wagairah pe.......aur agar advertisement cancel kar do to wo rajat kalia ka bachcha hota hai...... is cheez ko ad astra kehte hain.......... mere according to sirf advertisement dekhna hi hota hai usse bachcha paida ho jaata hai.......jaise golgappe khane se bhi bachcha paida hota hai........

rajat kalia itna comfortable life isliye jee raha tha kyonke use ads achchi lagti thi isliye itni zyada ads dekhta tha......... jabke manish ko ya to ads kahin dikhi nahi thi ya pata nahi kya chakkar tha ke usko golgappe khaane ko aur ads dekhne ko dono hi kam mile honge shaayad jo usko dikkat aayi........

matlab log pooch rahe hain ke kya golgappe khaane se jo bachcha paida hua aur ad dekhne se jo bachcha paida hua un dono bachchon mein aapas mein kuch farak tha kya???? ab yeh to pata nahi mujhe waqt aane pe hi pata chalega ya ad aur golgappe ka farak rajat kalia ke broadcast mein samajhna padega ke kya difference ya similarity hai.......

Matlab sirf ek hi major farak hota hai ke ad dekhne se paida hone waala bachcha prepaid hota hai jabke golgappe khaane se paida hone waala bachcha postpaid hota hai......... advertisement ko india mein mashhuri aur foreign mein commercial bhi kehte hain isi tarah golgappe ko mumbai mein panipuri bhi kehte hain.........

ad de bhi sakte hain kahin to jo jo wo ad dekhega uske yahan ad dene waala bachcha ban ke paida hoga...... usi tarah golgappa ban bhi sakte hain to jo jo wo golgappa kheyega uske jo golgappa bana tha wo paida hoga.......... 

ad dene mein paisa lagta hai jabke golgappa banna ek physical effort ka kaam hai......
